About this site

This site is a public record of where I stand on common questions about the Christian faith. The goal is not to win arguments — it's to be honest, consistent, and easy to reference.

Each entry is a question, a short summary of my position, and the reasoning behind it. I'll update entries as I learn, and I'll mark what I'm uncertain about as uncertain.

Plain answers. Held loosely where I'm uncertain, firmly where I'm not.
