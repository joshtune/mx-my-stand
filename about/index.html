About this site

I am a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. This site is a public record of where I stand on common questions about the Christian faith, written from that lens. The goal is not to win arguments — it's to be honest, consistent, and easy to reference.

Each entry is a question, a short summary of my position, and the reasoning behind it. I'll update entries as I learn, and I'll mark what I'm uncertain about as uncertain.

Every position on this site sits on a foundation I can't argue you into: a personal witness from the Lord that the Book of Mormon is the word of God, and that the Church of Jesus Christ has been restored through Joseph Smith. The articles explain the details. The foundation is personal revelation, available to anyone who asks (James 1:5-6).

Plain answers. Held loosely where I'm uncertain, firmly where I'm not.
