Where I stand

I am a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. These are my positions on common questions about the Christian faith — each entry a question, my honest answer, and the reasoning behind it.

  • Why do you need the Book of Mormon if you have the Bible?
    Because God promised more witnesses, and He kept the promise — the Book of Mormon stands alongside the Bible as another testament of Jesus Christ, not in place of it.
    scripturebook-of-mormon
  • How do you know the Book of Mormon is true?
    Because I asked the Lord — honestly, with an open mind, on the validity of its origin — and He answered. That witness is the foundation under every other position on this site.
    book-of-mormontestimonyfoundation
  • Do you believe the Bible is the Word of God?
    Yes — I hold Scripture as divinely inspired and authoritative, while taking its genres, history, and translation seriously.
    scriptureauthority
